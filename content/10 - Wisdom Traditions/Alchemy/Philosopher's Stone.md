The legendary alchemical substance said to be capable of turning base metals into gold, and produce the Elixir of Life which grants immortality.

[[20 - The Human System/Archetypes/Carl Jung]] saw this as a metaphor for the internal process of individuation, otherwise known as [[10 - Wisdom Traditions/Alchemy/The Great Work]].
