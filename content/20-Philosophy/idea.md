An eternally existing pattern that seeks manifestation through [[awareness]] and receptivity. In Platonic thought, the archetype of which individual things in any class are imperfect copies.

Ideas do not originate from within us but arrive from outside - through the realm of potential. As Rick Ruben suggests:

>If you have an idea you're excited about and you don't bring it to life, it's not uncommon for the idea to find its voice through another [[000 - Core Ideas/Creator\|maker]]. This isn't because the other artist stole your idea, but because the idea's time has come.

They represent the *what* behind manifestation—the pattern awaiting recognition rather than construction.

The [[000 - Core Ideas/Creative Process]] transforms ideas from potential into actuality. The [[000 - Core Ideas/Creator]] serves not as generator but as witness and vessel - recognizing what seeks to emerge rather than forcing it into being.
