An [[20 - The Human System/Archetypes/Archetype]] who's job it is to manage external impressions, respond to judgments, and govern self-presentation. Not in the lens of "faking it", but in terms of establishing success in relationships to others and selectively choosing to emphasize or downplay certain elements of personality relative to the environment.

This is my own take on what Jung called the Persona, derived from the latin word for the mask worn by actors in classical theatre. While the Persona may be the mask, the Stage Manager is both designing new masks and determining which one is applicable in each context.
